<div class="jumbotron-tagline">Journey to the magical world of Ea.</div>

1027 AR is an exciting and challenging time for the Illuminators, a group led by a mysterious figure known only as the Shrike in their search for ancient and mysterious artifacts of the lost and doomed nation of Kulthus. Banished to the Desolation, the Illuminators must work together and with the residents of the nearby town of Khotea to build up better than ever.

<hr>

Emblem of Ea is an epic fantasy MUSH set in the original world of Ea and has existed in various incarnations since 2002. We have updated to the AresMUSH codebase and the Pathfinder 2e ruleset.
